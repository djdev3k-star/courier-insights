## Platform Algorithm Tips

The courier platforms (Uber, DoorDash, etc.) use algorithms that favor:

1. **Consistency** - Same work hours every week = better order assignments
2. **Reliability** - Acceptance rate >95% = priority orders
3. **Responsiveness** - Low cancellation rate = more order offers
4. **Rating** - Stay above 4.9 stars = surge orders
5. **Velocity** - Faster completion = more orders queued

**This schedule is DESIGNED to maximize all of these.**

## Signs You Should Quit a Shift Early

- You've completed your daily target in 2 hours and it's low-demand time

- Your phone shows 0 active orders AND there's no incoming

**Don't just sit idle.** Go home, charge, prep for next shift.
